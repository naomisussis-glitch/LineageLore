🌲 Family Tree (GEDCOM file)
Export your family tree from Ancestry.com, MyHeritage, or FamilySearch as a .ged file, then upload it here. The AI will read all the names, dates, and places automatically.
On Ancestry: go to your tree → click the three-dot menu → Export Tree → Download GEDCOM.
Note: Ancestry.com links to your tree can't be read directly (they're private), so the GEDCOM export is the best way to share your tree.

🧪 DNA / Ethnicity Results
Upload a screenshot or photo of your ethnicity results from Ancestry or 23andMe — the AI will read the image and extract your breakdown. You can also upload a text file of your results if you've saved one.
Tip: On 23andMe, go to Ancestry → Ancestry Composition. On Ancestry, go to DNA → Ethnicity Estimate. Take a screenshot and upload it here.
Note: Raw DNA data files (the large rsID files) contain genetic markers that can't be interpreted for ancestry without specialist software — please upload a screenshot of your ethnicity estimate page instead.
